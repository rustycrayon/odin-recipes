Chicken Stir Fry

Description

This chicken stir fry recipe is the perfect quick fix for a weeknight meal. With only chicken and broccoli as the main ingredients, you can cook a delicious stir fry in no time flat! Serve with rice for a healthy, asian meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Broccoli
  • Onion
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame seed oil
  • Cornstarch
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Coat the chicken in cornstarch, then cook till light brown
  2. Add aromatics like onion, garlic, and ginger
  3. Cook the broccoli in the pan at high heat
  4. Make the sauce while the broccoli cooks
  5. Add the chicken to the broccoli and coat with the sauce
