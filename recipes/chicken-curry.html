Chicken Curry

Description

Perfect for cold nights or the spicy food lover, this chicken curry dish is packed with flavors and aroma. Enjoy this Indian staple with a creamy base and side of basmati rice or naan.

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken breasts
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 3 tablespoons of curry
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste
  • 1 cup of yogurt
  • 3/4 cup of coconut milk
  • 1/2 lemon squeezed
  • 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Saut√© onion with oil and garlic
  2. Add spices
  3. Add chicken, tomato paste, yogurt and coconut milk; bring to a boil
  4. Stir in lemon juice and cayenne pepper
  5. Serve with rice and enjoy!
