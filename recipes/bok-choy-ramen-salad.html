Bok Choy Ramen Salad

Description

Mixed with fresh greens, crunchy almonds, green onions, and ramen, this light salad delivers a wealth of texture. Enjoy this recipe as a quick meal in the afternoon or pair it with your favorite main dish!

Ingredients

  • Vinaigrette dressing
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 packages of ramen
  • 3 ounces of almonds
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 2 heads of bok choy
  • 6 green onions, chopped

Steps

  1. Melt butter in saucepan over low heat, add sugar
  2. Place ramen in butter mixture
  3. Stir in ramen seasoning, almonds, and soy sauce
  4. Cook until lightly toasted
  5. Toss with greens and serve!
